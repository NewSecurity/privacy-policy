User Experience Program Statement

Welcome to the New Security User Experience Program!

In order to improve your user experience of New Security, we release this User Experience Program to our user. In this program, we will conduct statistical analysis of how users interact with the product and each of its functions. Through this analysis, we are able to continuously optimize your user experience and the performance of New Security, as well as to improve functionality and introduce innovative security features that benefit users. This User Experience Program will never collect users’ personal information. And take part in this program is your own choice when you launch New Security.

New Security considers your privacy is significant and important. That’s why we provide this statement to explain the User Experience Program.


The Program collects the following types of information:

  1. 1. Basic application experience statistics:

In order to attentively respond to the development of new malware and viruses on different mobile platforms, as well as to maximize satisfaction of primary user needs, we strive to continue to improve our product functionality. This is why we collect statistics on the use of each function; how the app behaves in consideration of its performance, such as how long the junk is scanned, how much junk is scanned and what is being scanned. This represents the number of users for a particular function.

  1. 2. Feature usage statistics during A/B Test:

Statistics during A/B Test period about how you use our app, including what the actions you take when using the app such as buttons you click on in the app, how frequently you use a certain feature, to what extent a feature is being used, and so on.


  1. 3. Non-Personal information:

we will collect your non-personal information, including your device information, phone model, logs, operation system, Android ID, CPN model, memory size, your phone IMEI number, rom, installed application name and package name, app md5 value, location, install, uninstall, frequency of use, and so on. Only for functional supporting.

How your information is used:

Data collected pursuant to Program will be used only for the purpose of analyzing, evaluating, reviewing and improving the user experience of our product and service.We will upload your installed apps information to https://sla-intl.trustlook.com/v2/query to save for analysis. This address is our cooperate website. This is for your phone security. And we will not use this information for other commercial purposes, unless we obtain your consent.

Information helps us to more accurately determine the importance of each function, and guides us in adjusting its priority for research and development. During this process, we only analyze the usage frequency statistics of product functions, and this will never involve users’ personal data.


The security of the information:

We are very concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. We take precautions – including administrative, physical and electronic measures to protect data from unauthorized access. Please be aware that no security measure that we take to protect your information is absolutely guaranteed to avoid unauthorized access or use of information which is impenetrable.
